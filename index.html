Good to know

Candle care

Should I trim the wick?

Always trim to about 5mm before lighting — it prevents smoking and helps the scent carry evenly through the room.

How long should I burn it the first time?

Let the wax pool reach the edges of the jar on the first burn, usually 2-3 hours. This sets an even burn for the candle's whole life.

What's the best way to put it out?

Use a snuffer or dip the wick into the wax pool and straighten it, rather than blowing — this avoids spatter and keeps the wick ready for next time.

About us

A room changes the moment a candle is lit

The light softens. The air carries something warm. For a few minutes, the day slows down enough to notice it. That small shift — from ordinary to unhurried — is the entire reason Sia Scented Candles exists. We make handcrafted soy wax scented candles, poured in small batches, for people who understand that a home isn't just decorated — it's felt.

Our Story

Sia didn't begin as a business plan. It began the way most honest things do — quietly, at a kitchen table, with wax, wicks, and no real idea of where it would lead.

What started as a way to bring a little more calm into one home slowly became a craft. Each candle was tested, re-poured, adjusted — not for scale, but for feel. Does the scent unfold the right way in a small room? Does the flame stay steady? Does it still feel special on the fifth burn as it did on the first?

That instinct — care over convenience — is still how every candle is made today. Sia stayed small on purpose. Small batches mean nothing sits in a warehouse losing its character before it reaches you. It means a real hand shaped the wax you're about to light.

Why Soy Wax?

Not every candle is made the same way, and that difference matters more than people realize. Sia candles are poured from 100% natural soy wax — no paraffin, no petroleum by-products, nothing that clouds the air you breathe. Soy wax burns cleaner and slower, which means your candle lasts longer and the fragrance stays true instead of turning sharp or synthetic halfway through.

It's also biodegradable and renewable — a small, quiet choice that adds up. Choosing a natural candle isn't about following a trend. It's about not having to choose between something beautiful and something that's actually good for your home.

Our Craftsmanship

Every Sia candle is hand-poured, start to finish. Fragrance oils are premium and carefully balanced — not to overpower a room, but to sit in it comfortably, the way a good scent should.

Cotton and wooden wicks are chosen for a clean, even burn, so the wax pools evenly instead of tunneling down the middle and wasting half the candle. This is what handcrafted candles are supposed to mean: nothing rushed, nothing mass-produced, every jar finished by someone who checked it before it left the table.

Our Commitment to Sustainability

Luxury and responsibility aren't opposites. At Sia, they're the same decision. From the natural, biodegradable wax to thoughtful, minimal packaging, every choice is made with the same question in mind: does this leave the smallest footprint it can, without leaving out the experience?

Eco-friendly candles shouldn't feel like a compromise — they should simply feel like the obvious way to do this.
